Welcome to the East Kew United Soccer Club homepage

East Kew United Soccer Club is a family oriented club with over 30 years of history in the Kew area. Established in 1985 and based at the Willsmere Park, East Kew, we aim to strike a balance between competitiveness, healthy sport and social enjoyment.


Our key principles are:


  • Treat people involved in our sporting activities with courtesy, respect and proper regard for their rights.
  • Support the club for members and community well being.
  • Recognising we are part of the community, behave in a manner that does upholds standing and reputation of the club.

Our family based club is as much about social interaction as competition. With many of us coming to the end of our playing careers, we welcome new members, young and old to join our club.


We participate in the Victorian Amateur Soccer Federation with our games scheduled on Sunday mornings and training on Wednesday evenings.


For further information or to join our club please contact a member of our committee.
